## Caveats (honest)

- AE catalog is dominated by generic/Chinese OEM brands. MSI Suprim, ASUS ROG, Corsair flagships, AMD Ryzen 9, Intel Core i9 are **not sold on AE** — they're distributor-only (PCDiga / Globaldata / Megasur in PT). Absence of AE top-10 for those categories is expected, not a gap.
